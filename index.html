ABOUT ME

Capturing Moments


I've always loved capturing the small moments and interactions in life and in people. I think that seeing these unnoticed interactions captured in a moment in time is one of the most beautiful things you can see. Photos also often bring out the truest moments in people and nature alike; they give us a glimpse into who people are and what nature has to offer. I think that that is what I like most about photography. Photos often capture the most innocent, natural, and unfiltered reactions.
